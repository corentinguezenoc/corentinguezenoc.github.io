Corentin Guezenoc is a French composer for video games and films based in Montréal, Québec, Canada. With expertise in sound design, in-game audio implementation, and programming, he seamlessly integrates his compositions into immersive experiences.

In another life, Corentin is an audio signal processing engineer with a PhD, which makes him well at ease with all things technical, including programming in C++ and Python.
